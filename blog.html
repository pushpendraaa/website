Our Certifcate & Online Programs for 2022

VIT Bhopal has made a mark in higher education as one of the best engineering institutes in Central India. The Institute hosts 6 Schools offering academic and research-oriented engineering and management courses both at undergraduate and post-graduate levels. The prospective students can opt for any school, based on their area of interest and future career goals.
At VIT Bhopal, research plays an imperative role on par with academics, facilitating interdepartmental collaborations, besides providing ample opportunities for the students to keep themselves updated of the technological advancements and innovations. All the schools are equipped with state of the art laboratories to impart practical knowledge as per the current industrial standards.
VIT Bhopal has emerged as one of the best engineering Institutes in Central India which offers new innovative under graduate and post graduate courses to keep pace with the changing technology scenario. The futuristic infrastructure, novel teaching learning methodologies, excellent academics have added to the accomplishments. VIT Bhopal assures its students an exposure to cutting edge technology and provides them with immense career opportunities in terms of better placements in India and abroad, or gives them opportunities to pursue higher education in the top ranked universities of the world.
Envisioning the best for every student, by giving them the “VIT edge” and providing every student a place to learn and a chance to grow; is the main goal of VIT Bhopal. The educational experience is enriched by the cultural diversity, with faculty and students, hailing from different parts of the country.
